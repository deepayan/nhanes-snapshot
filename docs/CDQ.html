National Health and Nutrition Examination Survey

1999-2000 Data Documentation, Codebook, and Frequencies

Cardiovascular Health (CDQ)

Data File: CDQ.xpt

First Published: June 2002
Last Revised: NA

Component Description

The Cardiovascular section (prefix CDQ) provides personal interview data on symptoms that may be associated with congestive heart failure.

Eligible Sample

All participants 40 year of age and older are asked these questions.
