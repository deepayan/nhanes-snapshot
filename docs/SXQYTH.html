National Health and Nutrition Examination Survey

1999-2000 Data Documentation, Codebook, and Frequencies

Sexual Behavior - Youth (SXQYTH)

RDC Only Data File: SXQYTH.xpt

First Published: September 2018
Last Revised: NA
Due to disclosure issues, this dataset is not available publicly. Access may be obtained through the NCHS Research Data Center (https://www.cdc.gov/rdc).

Component Description

The ACASI section on sexual behavior (variable name prefix SXQ) provides information on lifetime and current sexual behavior for both men and women. Major items in this section include age of first sexual intercourse, number of sexual partners, use of condoms, circumcision status (for men), history of sexually transmitted diseases. Youths aged 14-19 were administered a limited subset of questions asked of adults 20-59 years.

Eligible Sample

Sample persons aged 14-59 years were eligible. Several questions in this section are age and gender specific. Only data for youths aged 14-19 years are included in this release. Mentally impaired individuals, participants needing a proxy informant, or who were unable to comprehend English or Spanish were not asked these questions.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

These questions were administered as part of the NHANES Audio Computer Assisted Self Interview (ACASI) conducted in a private room in the MEC in either English or Spanish without the presence of an interviewer. The ACASI enabled the respondents both to hear questions through earphones and read questions on the computer and to move at their own speed as they touch the screen to indicate their response.  

Analytic Notes

Wording of some of the questions differed slightly between the 1999 and 2000 surveys. In the 1999 version, the term "sexual intercourse" was used (instead of "sex") in the following questions: SXQ030, SXQ120, SXQ130, SXQ150, SXQ170, SXQ190, SXQ200, SXQ220.
