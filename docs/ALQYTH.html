National Health and Nutrition Examination Survey

1999-2000 Data Documentation, Codebook, and Frequencies

Alcohol Use - Youth (ALQYTH)

RDC Only Data File: ALQYTH.xpt

First Published: January 2007
Last Revised: NA
Due to disclosure issues, this dataset is not available publicly. Access may be obtained through the NCHS Research Data Center (https://www.cdc.gov/rdc).

Component Description

This short section on alcohol use (ALQ) focused on age of first drink (initiation), lifetime and current use (past 30 days). Questions were not specific to type of alcohol used.

Eligible Sample

12-19 years.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The NHANES Audio Computer Assisted Interview (ACASI) was administered in a private room in the MEC. Interviews were conducted in English and Spanish. The ACASI enables respondents to hear questions through earphones as well as read the questions on the computer monitor. The respondents proceeded through the questions at their own speed; touch screens were used to select responses to the questions. No proxy respondents or translators were permitted.

Quality Assurance & Quality Control

Data collections software included consistency checks, including range checks, valid response checks, and checks for logical relationships. Soft edits alerted respondents to a potential error or problems, but allowed the respondents to proceed after confirming that the entry was correct. Hard edits prohibited the respondents from continuing with the ACASI until the error was corrected.

Data Processing and Editing

The reported answers to questions ALD030 and ALD040 have been edited for consistency with answers to preceding questions. If any respondent reported a greater number of days for ALD030 (During the past 30 days, on how many days did you have at least one drink of alcohol?) than for ALD020 (During your life, on how many days have you had at least one drink of alcohol?), ALD030 was set to the largest value consistent with the answer to ALD020. Similar if any respondent reported a greater number of days for ALD040 (During the past 30 days, on how many days did you have 5 or more drinks of alcohol in a row, that is, within a couple of hours?) than for either ALD020 or ALD030, ALD040 was set to the largest value consistent with the answers to ALD020 and ALD030.

Analytic Notes

Alcohol questions for respondents, ages 20+ years are also available.
